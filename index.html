About Me

I’m a junior QA with a sharp eye for detail and a genuine love of problem‑solving. My core strengths lie in manual and API testing across web and mobile: I plan and execute test suites, create clear STP / STD / STR documentation, and track issues in Jira, monday and TestRail.

I’m also building solid automation skills—writing starter scripts in Selenium‑WebDriver (Java / Python) and crafting REST collections in Postman. Curiosity drives me, quality inspires me, and I’m excited to bring that mindset to every product I test, and in my free time I enjoy learning about AI image‑design tools like Stable Diffusion, RunwayML, and more.
